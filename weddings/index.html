Good to Know

Wedding photo booth questions.

How much does a wedding photo booth cost?

Wedding packages start at $550 for a 3-hour open-air Classic Booth. The Party Glam Booth (our most popular) is $650, and the all-inclusive Big Kahuna — with a VIP red carpet, premium backdrop, and a live keychain or magnet station — is $1,300. Final pricing depends on hours, backdrop, add-ons, and travel.

What's included with the booth?

Every booth comes with a professional on-site attendant, unlimited 2x6 or 4x6 prints, a curated prop and backdrop setup, instant photo sharing by text and email, and a private online gallery after your event.

What is a keychain or magnet station?

These are our live-favor stations. Guests watch their photos become custom keychains (50 included) or acrylic magnets (50 included) on the spot, so they leave your wedding with a keepsake the same night.

Do you travel to our venue?

Yes. We're a service-area business and travel to venues throughout Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Naples, Marco Island, and the rest of Southwest Florida.

How fast do we get our photos?

Every photo is sent instantly by text and email during your wedding, and your full private online gallery is delivered within 48 hours.
