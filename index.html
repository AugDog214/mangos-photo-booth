Good to Know

Frequently asked questions.

How much does a photo booth rental cost in Fort Myers?

Mango’s packages start at $550 for our 3-hour Classic open-air booth. The Party Glam Booth (our most popular) is $650, and the all-inclusive Big Kahuna — with a live keychain or magnet station, a VIP red carpet, and custom décor — is $1,300. Keychain and magnet stations are also available as add-ons on any package. Your final price depends on add-ons, travel, and any custom requests.

What areas of Southwest Florida do you serve?

We serve Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Naples, Marco Island, and the surrounding Southwest Florida region (Lee and Collier counties). We’re a service-area business, so we travel to your venue.

How long is a photo booth rental?

Every Mango's package includes three hours of attended service. Additional hours are available on request when you check your date.

What’s included with a Mango’s photo booth?

Every booth comes with a professional on-site attendant, unlimited 2x6 or 4x6 prints, a curated prop and backdrop setup, instant photo sharing by text and email, and a private online gallery after your event.

How fast do we get our photos?

Every photo is sent instantly by text and email during your event, and your full private online gallery is delivered within 48 hours.

What is a keychain or magnet station?

These are our live-favor stations — included in the Big Kahuna package, or available to add to any booth. At the Live Keychain Station, guests watch their photos become custom keychains on the spot; the Magnet Station turns photos into acrylic photo magnets — so guests take a keepsake home the same night.

Do you do weddings and corporate events?

Yes. We specialize in weddings, corporate and brand activations, birthdays, quinceañeras, and holiday parties across Southwest Florida, with branded templates and custom backdrops available for corporate clients.
